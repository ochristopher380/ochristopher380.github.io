Washington State Ferries - On Time Performance Dashboard Redesign
Ever wonder if your ferry is going to be late and how long it might be delayed for? Through my redesign of the WSF dashboard, you can find answers in the data for yourself!
Project Details
I redesigned the Washington State Ferries On-Tim Performance Dashboard into three separate views to create a dashboard that allows users to understand delays for the ferry system. Each view reflects a different question that a user might have, from how the system is performing at a high level, what is happening on an individual route and what is the experience of a customer/passenger? My redesign uses clear and clean visuals to provide quick access to insight for those hoping to understand the ferry system or someone who simply wants to know what the likelihood of their boat being delayed.
