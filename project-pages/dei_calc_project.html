Included DEI Calculator
A DEI calculator to help DEI practitioners understand the trajectory of DEI in their organizations, scenario plan, and tell stories to get leadership buy-in.
Project Details
As a part of the completion of my Master's Degree in Information Managment, I worked with a team of my peers and Included.AI to develop a workforce planning tool for DEI practitioners to understand the trajectory of DEI in their organization as well as scenario plan and get leadership buy-in. We developed this tool as an agile project using Shiny for Python.
