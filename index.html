About Me
I am a skilled and passionate problem solver who uses data to produce impactful results. My goal as a data professional is to solve problems. That might mean extracting data that was not accessable in a source system, or through webscraping, and creating a dashboard, or training a predictive model. I have experience in both descriptive and predictive analytics, as well as automation, and am skilled with Python, Tableau, SQL, Alteryx and other tools.
I hold a Master's Degree in Information Management from the University of Washington and a Bachelor's in Political Science from the University of Michigan. I most recently was the Graduate Intern in the Business Intelligence Department at the Port of Seattle. Prior to the Port, I worked as a Paralegal where I developed automations with Power Automate and prepared reports using Excel.
